Prof. Xiangen Hu

Professor Xiangen Hu is a renowned Psychologist and Computer Scientist. He is the Chair Professor of Learning Sciences and Technologies at Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He received his Doctor of Philosophy degree in Cognitive Psychology from the University of California, Irvine in 1991 and 1993 respectively. He took up an Assistant Professorship at The University of Memphis in 1993 and was promoted to Associate Professor in 2000 and Professor in the Department of Psychology in 2009. Meanwhile, he has been appointed Prof and Dean of the School of Psychology in the Central China Normal University since 2016 on a visiting basis where he has established good connections with the Chinese mainland. He joined PolyU in Dec 2023 as Director of the Institute for Higher Education Research and Development. His research areas include Mathematical Psychology, Research Design and Statistics, and Cognitive Psychology.

Dr. Allen Dorcas

Dr. Allen Dorcas is a distinguished Counselling Psychologist and educator with over 25 years of experience in psychology and counselling. He holds a PhD in Psychology from The University of Hong Kong and a Master’s in Counselling Psychology from the University of Montreal. Since 1995, he has been teaching at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, where he has received multiple teaching excellence awards. Dr. Dorcas has also practiced as a Counselling Psychologist, working with diverse clients to foster resilience and self-discovery. With over 30 years of mindfulness practice, he integrates holistic approaches into his work, making him a trusted voice in mental wellness and a perfect fit for EmoBay’s mission.
